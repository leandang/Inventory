The Story of Vietnamese Pho

Pho originated from the North of Vietnam and popularized in the Central and the South in the years 1950 - 1955. Due to the division of the two regions, a part of the Northern people migrated to the South. and bring Pho to the South. In the years 1975, many overseas Vietnamese made Pho known to many parts of the world. Especially the Americas and Europe. And the Vietnamese people in this period were sent to Russia, Poland, ... to study and work, contributed to the transmission of this noodle dish to these countries.
